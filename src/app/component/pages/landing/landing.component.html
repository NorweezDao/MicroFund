About: GalapagosHub

The Galapagos is a unique archipelago, a World Heritage Site and one of the most important conservation areas in the world. It’s biodiversity inspired Charles Darwin's theory of evolution.

For our first round of funding, we have partnered with GalapagosHub and the ex-governor of the Galapagos Norman Wray to provide funding that will help restore and preserve the archipelago.
